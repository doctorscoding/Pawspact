At the heart of our business

  • Animal Welfare

    Pawspact app is meticulously designed to prioritize animal welfare by addressing a significant challenge faced by pet owners - the high cost of pet care. This financial burden often leads to difficult decisions and compromises the well-being of animals. Pawspact aims to reduce these costs and make pet care more affordable, promoting animal welfare in a profound way. By alleviating the financial burden, more pets can receive the care they need, leading to healthier, happier, and thriving animal companions.

  • Stories

    Amidst Pawspact's impact on animal welfare and pet care costs, we celebrate selfless animal rescuers. They tirelessly save animals from streets, paying from their pockets for treatments, TNR, vaccinations, and microchips. Their dedication transforms lives, like an emaciated stray cat now thriving in a loving home, and an abandoned dog wagging its tail with newfound joy. These heroes exemplify compassion's power, creating a better world for animals.

  • Adopt and Foster

    Opt for adoption and become a hero in a dog or cat's life. By choosing to adopt from our rescued animals or one of the numerous shelters and rescue groups across the country, you're not just welcoming a loyal and affectionate companion into your family; you're also making a life-saving impact. Save lives and find your perfect furry friend through adoption today.

  • Vet on a Mission

    Join our mission to provide essential veterinary care to animals in need. Our dedicated team of veterinarians travels to underserved areas, offering free medical services to pets and stray animals. With your support, we can make a significant impact on improving the health and well-being of animals everywhere.
